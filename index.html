Simulation & Quiz

Welding Simulator

The 3D Dynamic Welding Simulator is an educational tool designed for Grade 10 TLE (Technology and Livelihood Education) welding students at Pampanga High School. It offers a safe, immersive training environment that simulates real-life welding scenarios. Students can practice various welding techniques in a controlled, virtual setting, improving their skills without the risks or costs associated with traditional methods. This simulator is tailored to enhance practical learning while tracking student progress through interactive modules and performance metrics.

Quiz

The quiz in the 3D Dynamic Welding Simulator allows students to test their knowledge of welding techniques and safety protocols. Upon completion, the quiz scores are automatically sent to the teacher's web-based account, where they can track and assess student performance in real time. This system enables efficient monitoring and helps teachers provide targeted feedback based on each student's progress.
